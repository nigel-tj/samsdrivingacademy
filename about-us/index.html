about us

Sam’s Driving Academy is an independent, friendly & reliable driving Academy, providing friendly, professional driving tuition from experienced, qualified instructors. We have earned an excellent reputation for our patient, calm and considerate approach to driving tuition and we will do our best to help you relax and enjoy lessons and get you on the way to passing your driver’s test 1st time. Learning to drive can be a very exciting and rewarding experience, but it’s not something you want to last forever, here at SDA we tailor your lessons to your learning pace and individual need, getting you test ready at manageable rate and affordable price. We only teach at “one to one” basis and no lessons are quite the same. We see the” instructor-Student “relationship very much as a team effort and will help Students in an encouraging and nurturing manor to achieve their goal. Nervous drivers are always welcome and we use safe off road areas where you will be able to learn without the distraction or worry of other vehicle, so your first few lessons can be conducted quietly until you feel confident enough on a public road. Whether you’ve just received your learner’s license, or you’ve already had some driving lessons and been unsuccessful in passing your test, we will equip you with the skills and ability to handle all kinds of traffic conditions. Your safety, before and after is very important to us at Sam’s driving Academy, so we will not only teach you how to pass your practical driver’s test, but to make you a safe driver for life. All our lessons are conducted in small, comfortable easy to drive cars. All cars are fully insured and fitted with dual pedals for your safety. We all have our strengths and weaknesses, and when it comes to learning to drive, it is no different. In Fact that is one of the reasons we love teaching so much. Simply because everyone is different and therefore present a whole new set of challenges, right from the first lesson to passing their driving test.

OUR MISSION

  • To have the highest graduates of our programs with the safest diving records of any other key player in South Africa
  • To provide professional, informed, and comfortable instruction that turns new or inexperienced drivers into safe, collision-free, and confident drivers.
  • To create a fun environment for instructors and students that fosters valuable learning and hands-on experience.
