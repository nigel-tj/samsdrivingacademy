Our driving packages help new or inexperienced drivers in Washington, DC earn a driver’s license and/or make people improve behind the wheel. Our sessions are built to accommodate the unique requirements of every student and each lesson is customized to the actual progress demonstrated by the student.

Led by our professional and experienced instructors, these sessions deliver safe, confident, informed, and patient drivers to Washington, DC roads. Students learn the most effective defensive driving techniques and everything required to pass the road test administered by the Washington, DC Department of Motor Vehicles.

Regardless of whether you prefer 5 or 20 hours of instruction, you can be sure that our courteous, experienced, and patient instructors will help you succeed in your driving goals.

*For pick-up/drop-off sessions, any time beyond 10 minutes driving distance from our location will be deducted from the lesson time.

*Students are allowed to use a James Keller Driving School vehicle for their road test following successful completion of a mock road test administered by one of our instructors.
